Welcome To Dar Al Manasek Catering - Premier Catering services

For the past 20 years, Dar Al Manasek Catering has been creating innovative culinary experiences throughout Saudi Arabia. We have over twenty years of event planning expertise, so whether you are planning a corporate event, an elaborate served dinner or anything in between, our event coordinators can plan, design and staff your event. We are dedicated to the highest standard of professionalism and excellence.

Our renown chefs have utilized their culinary expertise to customize the perfect menus for our clients, ideal for the season. Over the years, they have been incorporating fresh new recipes with our high quality organic produce for economical prices. We operate our own Bakery where we have perfected our own breads, scones, muffins and desserts, using only the highest quality ingredients available.

Our ultimate goal at Dar Al Manasek Catering is to create an unforgettable event for each and every client. We look forward to serving you and we value your business.
